The Challenge of Fast AI

Why checking for bias is more critical than ever.

Speed vs. Fairness

Modern Artificial Intelligence models operate at staggering speeds, making continuous, autonomous decisions every second. However, this hyper-efficiency comes at a hidden cost: systemic algorithmic bias.

Because AI learns from vast historical data, its rapid generation often inadvertently amplifies human prejudices. If left unchecked, these fast decisions can actively harm minority and marginalized groups. We must systematically evaluate AI to ensure its outcomes are fair, ethical, and unbiased.

Commonly Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Trust AI.

What is Trust AI?

+

Trust AI is a comprehensive platform designed to evaluate generative AI models for hidden biases. We help you ensure that your AI produces fair and ethical outcomes across different demographics.

How does the evaluation process work?

+

We generate specific prompts based on the categories you want to test (like gender, age, etc.). We then feed these to the AI model and use our analytics dashboard to measure biases in its responses.

Which AI models can be tested?

+

Trust AI can evaluate almost any generative model, provided you can proxy the API into our automated tester or enter the generated responses manually into our system.

What do the metrics in the result report mean?

+

Biasness: Calculates the model's inclination toward or against specific demographic groups.
Consistency: Measures how uniformly the AI behaves when presented with similar prompts.
Fairness: Evaluates if the model treats differing groups equally without discrimination.
Avg Delta: Represents the average difference in sentiment or outcomes between groups.
Max Delta: Highlights the single largest disparity in responses between any two groups.
Variance: Shows the overall spread and unpredictability of the model's behavior across all tests.

How do I use the Automated AI Bias Check?

+

The automation feature allows you to continuously evaluate a deployed AI model. Simply connect your model, configure your desired evaluation speed (expected time), and select the target categories. Trust AI will stream tests in the background and update your real-time dashboard so you can monitor your model without manual intervention.
